The Duty of Care: Protecting Staff Wellbeing

The Problem with Legacy Systems

Institutions rely on student feedback, but legacy keyword dictionaries are critically inaccurate against modern linguistic complexities. They miss passive-aggressive, discriminatory, and nuanced abuse, exposing academic staff to psychological harm.

The AI Hybrid Solution

By implementing a Large Language Model (LLM) alongside traditional scanning, we successfully identify the threats that hardcoded rules miss.

Traditional Keyword Dictionary Catch Rate Limited
AI Semantic Scanning Catch Rate Comprehensive

*The AI analysis identified numerous instances of severe, nuanced abuse that the dictionary-only method completely missed, closing a significant gap in institutional risk management.
