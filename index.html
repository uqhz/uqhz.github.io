Professional Profile

I am a Senior Principal Data Analyst at the Institute for Teaching and Learning Innovation (ITaLI) at The University of Queensland, and a PhD Candidate in Data Science within the School of EECS.

My professional expertise lies in system migration, cloud infrastructure management, and developing AI-driven solutions for qualitative data analysis. Most recently, I led the technical integration of the university's new Blue evaluation system. I am actively developing and optimizing AI-driven sentiment analysis pipelines using large language models like Claude and Gemini to process rich student feedback.

My academic research explores data quality management, with a specific focus on the role of metadata in evaluating repurposed data.

AI Application & Architecture

Highlighting recent technical leadership in developing applied AI pipelines for academic quality assurance and institutional data governance.

SECaT NLP Pipeline

Designed a custom NLP pipeline using AWS Bedrock (Claude) to extract themes and analyze sentiment within rich qualitative student feedback.

Optimization: Engineered intelligent token batching (3,500 - 4,000 chars) to prevent JSON parsing failures and reduce API latency, ensuring high reliability for institutional reporting.

Abusive Language Detection

Engineered a hybrid moderation approach combining hardcoded dictionary screening with advanced semantic AI to automatically flag harmful language in student comments.

Impact: Ensures a safe, professional reporting environment for academic staff by pre-screening thousands of qualitative records.

Human-in-the-Loop Platform

Built an internal web-based review portal allowing administrative staff to securely audit AI-flagged comments, providing critical ethical oversight.

Governance: Enables precise classification tracking ("Dictionary Only," "AI Only," or "Both") to continually refine model accuracy.

Evaluation System Migration

Led the technical migration of the legacy student evaluation system to the new "Blue" platform. Currently architecting the migration of AI scripts to the UQ Data Lake using Airflow.

Scale: Streamlines university-wide course and teaching evaluations, informing executive strategic decision-making.
