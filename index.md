## PROFESSIONAL SUMMARY
I am a Senior Data Analyst at the Institute for Teaching and Learning Innovation (ITaLI) at The University of Queensland, and a PhD Candidate in Computer Science within the School of EECS. 

My professional expertise lies in system migration, cloud infrastructure management, and developing AI-driven solutions for qualitative data analysis. Most recently, I led the technical integration of the university's new Blue evaluation system. I am actively developing and optimizing AI-driven sentiment analysis pipelines using large language models like Claude and Gemini to process rich student feedback. 

Currently, I am preparing the dashboard for the SECaT (Student Evaluation of Course and Teaching) comment analysis pilot, while also architecting the migration of AI scripts and automation workflows to the UQ Data Lake utilizing Airflow. I hold an AWS Certified AI Practitioner certification, underpinning my focus on bridging complex data engineering with secure, scalable, and actionable academic quality assurance platforms.

My academic research explores data quality management, with a specific focus on the role of metadata in evaluating repurposed data.
